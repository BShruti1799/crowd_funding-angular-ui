Our Story

Founded in 2002 by visionary filmmaker and cultural advocate Mr. Dadasaheb Jagtap, the MCDC Trust has grown into a beacon of excellence in the realms of spiritual, cultural, and social media production.

What began as a passion project has evolved into a trusted institution dedicated to creating content that uplifts minds, honors tradition, and promotes social responsibility.

At MCDC, we believe that media is more than entertainment—it's a vehicle for transformation. Through carefully crafted films, documentaries, festivals, and educational programs, we aim to inspire individuals, spark dialogue, and drive community change.

"Our work reflects the rich cultural fabric of India, infused with universal values that transcend language and borders."
